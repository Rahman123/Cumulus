Relationships Related Classes

Relationships functionality falls into three main areas:

  • reciprocal Relationship record creation and management
  • automatic Relationship creation based on Lookup fields
  • the Relationships viewer

The classes involved with Relationships are:

  • REL_Relationships_TDTM
  • REL_Relationships_Cm_TDTM
  • REL_Relationships_Con_TDTM
  • REL_RelationshipsViewer_CTRL
  • REL_Relationships_TEST
  • REL_ProcessControl
  • REL_Utils
